# Welcome to my Git

## About me:
I come from a non-technical background - I started my career in international development - but I was always interested in data. Even in a seemingly non-technical field, I held roles in data and research. After the international development sector was destroyed by a certain administration, I decided to delve into the more technical aspects of data work and have been self-studying the tools that I already knew on a deeper level and learning newer, more advanced tools. 

I work with Excel, BigQuery and Looker in the Google space, Tableau, different types of SQL platforms (SQLite, MySQL, PostgreSQL), and dbt. 

## My data interests:
My personal interest in data is data related to user behavior (or human behavior in general) and trying to decipher the reasons behind specific behavioral trends, connecting the data to events in real life. I guess once a researcher, always a researcher.

In addition, after discovering dbt, I realized that I am particularly interested in the field of analytics engineering, as you have much more control over the data that you get in the end and the modeling of data is fascinating.
